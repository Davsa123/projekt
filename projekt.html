Jermaine Lamarr Cole, more famous as J. Cole, is an american rapper and reocrd producer.
He was born on January 28 1985 in a military base in Germany, but he was raised in North Carolina. He's quite tall and stands at 1.91 meters. Cole initially gained attention as a rapper following the release of his debut mixtape - The Come Up in the early 2007. Cole began rapping at the age of 12 because of his mom's Christmas gift. After that he realised he wanted to be a rapper and he saw it as an ideal profession.
